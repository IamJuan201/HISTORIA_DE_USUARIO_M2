Felix

Felix is a very playful and curious black cat. He loves exploring every corner of the house, chasing anything that moves and purring when he is happy. At night he is the king of the sofa.

Max

Max is a golden retriever full of energy and love. He is always the first to greet you when you get home, never says no to a walk and his tail never stops moving. A loyal and unconditional friend.

Pepe

Pepe is an Amazon parrot with bright feathers and a unique personality. He loves taking long naps, but when he is awake he watches everything very carefully. Calm, curious and very expressive in his own way.

Luna

Luna is a rabbit with long ears and a sweet look. She is very active at sunrise and sunset, she loves jumping in the grass and hiding in small places. Always alert but very calm with the people she knows.

Rocky

Rocky is a golden hamster with always full cheeks and an adorable look. He spends his days running on his wheel, storing seeds in his cheeks and exploring his cage with endless energy. Small but with a big personality.

Tito

Tito is a land turtle with a strong shell and endless patience. He enjoys sunbathing in the mornings, walking slowly through the garden and eating fresh leaves. He is never in a hurry and that is what makes him special.
